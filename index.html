DIVE WITH US

Learn how to dive or enjoy some amazing dive experiences… The Riviera Maya has it all!.

Visit the shallow and deep Reefs of Playa Del Carmen and Cozumel, explore the inside of Shipwrecks, challenge yourself with some Cavern or Cave diving in the magical Cenotes around Tulum, experience Bull Sharks in their natural habitat, venture out to see how the reef comes alive at Night or swim with the Whale Sharks, the biggest fish in the world!!

For you, ocean lover, we also offer Freediving and Kitesurfing lessons!

We will make you feel at home, welcoming you into our diving family in a relaxing and friendly atmosphere!
